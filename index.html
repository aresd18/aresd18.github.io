About

I am a physics student at the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). My interests include classical mechanics, fluid dynamics, aerospace systems and quantum physics.

Currently I participate in projects involving small satellite platforms such as CanSat systems, where I study aerodynamic behavior, descent dynamics and experimental data analysis.

Projects

CanSat Project

Development of a small satellite payload system designed for atmospheric descent experiments. The project includes sensor integration, data analysis and aerodynamic modeling of descent dynamics.

Physics Simulations

Numerical simulations of dynamical systems such as motion under drag forces and simplified fluid dynamics models using computational tools.

Research Notes

Aerodynamic Analysis of CanSat Descent

Preliminary analysis of drag forces and stability in small atmospheric descent payloads.

Conceptual study of thrust generation and flow behavior in small-scale propeller systems.
