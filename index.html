About

I am a physics student at the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). My academic interests include classical mechanics, fluid dynamics, aerospace systems and quantum physics.

Currently I am involved in projects related to small-scale aerospace systems such as CanSat platforms, where I study descent dynamics, aerodynamic behavior and experimental data analysis.

My long-term goal is to contribute to research in physics and engineering through experimental and computational approaches.
